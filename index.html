Bot Ranking Formula

Bots are ranked based on a weighted score calculated from live performance metrics. Each metric is normalized to a 0-100 scale before applying weights.

Score = (0.25 * Norm. Monthly Return) + (0.25 * Norm. Sharpe Ratio) + (0.20 * Norm. Calmar Ratio) + (0.10 * Norm. Time in Market) - (0.10 * Norm. Max Drawdown) - (0.10 * Norm. Recovery Time)
